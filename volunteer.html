Get Involved

Become a Buddy
& Mentor

Make a real difference in someone's life

We are building a compassionate community where neurodivergent young people and adults can thrive — and we need caring volunteers to make it happen. Join our Buddy & Mentorship Programme and become the support someone has been waiting for.

Why volunteer with us?

Meaningful Impact

Make a real and lasting difference in a neurodivergent young person's confidence, independence, and life.

Gain Valuable Experience

Build transferable skills in mentoring, empathy, communication and leadership — great for any career path.

Training Provided

All volunteers receive training and ongoing support to feel confident in their role. No prior experience needed.

Join a Community

Become part of a like-minded network working towards inclusion, empowerment, and real positive change.

How it works

1
Apply

Fill in our volunteer form below. We welcome applicants aged 18+ who are patient, empathetic, and open-minded.

2
Training

Receive training on neurodivergence awareness and how to support your mentee effectively.

3
Matching

We carefully pair you with a neurodivergent individual of a similar age to foster a natural connection.

4
Begin

Start your flexible sessions — in person, online, or both. Ongoing support from our team throughout.
