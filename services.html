Our Services

How we support you

From advocacy and mentorship to creative workshops — our programmes are designed around the real needs of neurodivergent individuals and their families.

Parent, Carer & Individual Support
Navigating the system — together

We walk alongside families and neurodivergent individuals in navigating the complex systems of education, health, and employment. We help with special needs applications, attend school or multi-agency meetings, and ensure families can access the services they need promptly and confidently. For young adults, we extend our support into college and workplace settings.

Help with special needs (EHCP) applications
School and multi-agency meeting attendance
Access to community services & resources
Support for young adults in college & work
Buddy & Mentorship Programme
Buddy up. Build confidence. Thrive together.

Our Buddy & Mentorship Programme pairs neurodivergent young adults and adults (ages 14–35) with neurotypical peer mentors of a similar age. Each mentor-buddy provides friendship, encouragement, and guidance — a balanced mix of companionship and support across personal, social, and professional areas.

Carefully matched neurotypical peer mentors
Confidence & independence building
Social & communication skills support
Education & career pathway guidance
To register, email [email protected] or use our contact form. Referrals from partner organisations also welcome.
Creative & Skills Development Workshops
Unlocking Creativity

We believe every individual deserves the opportunity to explore their talents and build skills for the future. Our workshops are tailored to the needs of the neurodivergent community — short, practical, and engaging — making learning accessible and enjoyable.
